Our Story

Founded in 2010 by Advocate Rajesh Kumar Sharma, File My Complaint was born from a simple belief: every citizen, regardless of economic status, deserves quality legal representation.

Over 15 years, we have handled more than 2,400 cases across consumer protection, labour disputes, property matters, and criminal defence — with an industry-leading 94% resolution rate.

Our digital platform was launched to remove the barriers that prevent ordinary people from accessing the legal system. Filing a complaint should not require a law degree.

"We don't just file paperwork. We fight for people."

— Adv. Rajesh Kumar Sharma, Founder
